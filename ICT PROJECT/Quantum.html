Quantum Computing with Microsoft

What is Quantum Computing?

Quantum computing is a revolutionary approach that harnesses the principles of quantum mechanics to process information in unprecedented ways. At Microsoft, we're pioneering quantum research to unlock solutions for complex global challenges, extending the reach of what's possible beyond classical computing.

Microsoft Quantum Projects

  • Q# Programming: A language tailored for quantum algorithms, providing developers the tools to build powerful quantum-based applications.
  • Azure Quantum: Azure's quantum platform brings quantum computing to developers worldwide, offering access to leading quantum hardware and resources.
  • Quantum Impact: Microsoft's quantum research extends into real-world applications such as materials science, cryptography, and beyond.
